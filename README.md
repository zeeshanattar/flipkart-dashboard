# 1) project title : flipkart sales analytics
flipkart sales analytics : A dynamic, interactive data visualization tool typically designed to give a bussiness team a 360 degree view of marketplace performance and to explore on regional comparison which can suit different attributes , complexity and suitability of ecommerce business platforms.
Short description/ purpose

# 2) Description / purpose 
the flipkart analytics is a visually engaging and analytical power bi report designed to help users explore and compare the sales fluctuation, the sales trend , sales fluctuations in different regions and cities in various product categories , payment modes. The dashboard focuses on highlighting major sales distribution and the marking of the peaks and the downfall over the monthly and quarterly basis. This tool is intended for use by different business analyst for ecommerce platform to understand trend and characteristics of the sales comming from different categories , mark the problem and take the data driven decisions.

# 3) Tech Stack
List of key technologies used to build the dashboard.
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

Power BI Desktop: Main data visualization platform used for report creation.

Power Query: Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing the data.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Used for calculated measures, dynamic visuals, and conditional logic.

Data Modeling: Relationships established among tables to enable cross-filtering and aggregation.

File Format: .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews

# 4) Data source
This dashboard is built using sample/synthetic e-commerce data modeled to represent Flipkart-like operations.

# 5) Features / Highlights: Dashboard Explation


# 5.1) Business problem :
       To mark the reason for the considerable downfall of the sales and the profit in the month of april to july.
   
# 5.2) Goal of the dashboard : 
       To design an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes Flipkart sales data to track performance, identify trends, and support data-driven business decisions.

# 5.3) walk through key visuals :
        The purpose of making an “Amount by Category” donut chart is to visually show how total sales (amount) are distributed across different product categories in a clear and intuitive    way and to visualize the proportion of total sales amount contributed by each product category.

        The purpose of making an “Amount by Payment Mode” pie chart is to show how total sales revenue is distributed across different payment methods, helping businesses understand customer payment preferences and to visualize the proportion of total sales amount generated through each payment mode.

        The purpose of making a “Top 5 Profitable Subcategories” clustered bar chart is to compare and highlight the subcategories that generate the highest profit, making it easy to identify the most valuable segments of the business and to compare and visualize the top 5 subcategories generating the highest profit.

        The purpose of making an “Amount vs Profit per Quarter” clustered column chart is to compare sales amount and profit across quarters to evaluate financial performance and profitability trends over time and to compare sales amount and profit across quarters in order to analyze financial performance and profitability trends.

        The purpose of making a “Monthly Distribution of Amount” stacked area chart is to analyze sales trends over time while showing how different categories (or segments) contribute to the total sales amount each month and to visualize monthly sales trends and the contribution of different categories to the total sales amount over time.

        The purpose of making a “Geographical Distribution by State” map is to visualize sales performance across different states, helping identify regional patterns, strengths, and opportunities and to visualize and analyze sales distribution across states in order to identify regional performance and business opportunities.

        The purpose of making a treemap of the Top 7 Cities is to visually compare sales performance among the highest-contributing cities and understand their relative impact on total sales to visualize and compare the sales contribution of the top 7 cities and identify key urban markets driving revenue.

        The purpose of making a KPI card for Amount is to display the total sales amount clearly and instantly, giving a quick snapshot of overall business performance to display the total sales amount as a key performance indicator for quick and effective performance monitoring.

       The purpose of making a KPI card for Profit is to show the total profit clearly and instantly, helping measure business profitability at a glance and to display the total profit as a key performance indicator for monitoring overall business profitability.

       The purpose of making a KPI card for Quantity is to quickly display the total number of products sold, giving a clear snapshot of sales volume and to display the total quantity of products sold as a key performance indicator for monitoring sales volume.

       The purpose of making a KPI card for Customers is to quickly show the total number of unique customers, providing an instant view of the customer base and reach and to display the total number of customers as a key performance indicator for monitoring customer reach and growth.

       The purpose of making a slicer for City is to allow users to filter and analyze sales data by specific cities, making the dashboard interactive and focused and to filter and analyze sales data by city, enabling interactive and region-specific insights.

       The purpose of making a slicer for Order Date is to allow users to filter and analyze sales data over specific time periods, enabling temporal insights and trend analysis and to filter and analyze sales data by order date, enabling interactive exploration of trends and period-specific performance.
